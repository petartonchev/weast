Welcome to MoodStock
What is MoodStock?
MoodStock monitors Twitter activity relating to top companies stock and computes the stock sentiment using state of the art machine learning technology.
How should I use MoodStock?
Select the stock you are interested in using and view the sentiment of your selected stock or portfolio.
Is MoodStock free?
For now MoodStock is 100% free!
Who are the creators of MoodStock?
We are a team of Data Scientists, Analysts and Domain experts who met in the University of Barcelona Masters in Foundations of Data Science.
